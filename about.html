Sweet potatoes are perennial herbs of the family Caenorhabditis elegans, especially when referred to as food or crop. It was considered a traditional rhubarb crop along with potatoes because it is rich in carbohydrates and is resistant to pests, and it is a vegetable that is widely loved today for its rich sweetness. Sweet potatoes are used not only for food, but also for industrial purposes for sugar, pharmaceuticals, and cosmetics production.
